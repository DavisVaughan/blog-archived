Which RStudio blog posts “pleased” Hadley? A tidytext + webscraping analysis

August 16, 2017

Introduction Awhile back, I saw a conversation on twitter about how Hadley uses the word “pleased” very often when introducing a new blog post (I couldn’t seem to find this tweet anymore. Can anyone help?). Out of curiousity, and to flex my R webscraping muscles a bit, I’ve decided to analyze the 240+ blog posts that RStudio has put out since 2011. This post will do a few things: Scrape the RStudio blog archive page to construct URL links to each blog post Scrape the blog post text and metadata from each post Use a bit of tidytext for some exploratory analysis Perform a statistical test to compare Hadley’s use of “pleased” to the other blog post authors Spoiler alert: Hadley uses “pleased” ALOT.
